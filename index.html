Track your daily water intake with WaterMinder.

Quickly log your water intake with the Apple Watch app

No need for taking out your iPhone from the pocket. Simply log the water from your wrist directly via the notifications! Additionally, inside the app you can see your water consumption at a glance, view a 2 week history graph and your achievements.

Complications are also available. In a simple, round progress ring, see how close you are towards your daily goal. This feature will keep you motivated!

Helping you to stay hydrated during your busy day

WaterMinder will motivate you to drink more water and stay hydrated. Start feeling better!

Create a Healthy Habit

It only takes 21 days to create a healthy habit. WaterMinder will remind you with friendly notifications to reach your daily hydration goals!

Simple To Use

Water tracking can't get easier than this! Get reminded, drink water. Tap button, log ounces. Wait until next reminder, repeat, hydrate.

Track Your Progress

View history of your water intake. See which days you met your goal, and which you didn't. Analyze your logged data in different time frames.

Rich Customizations

Configure custom notifications, create custom cup sizes, change notification sounds, calculate your own daily water intake goal and more!

Earn Achievements

Get some motivation and collect all of the WaterMinder badges when you drink more water. Share your hydration success with your friends!

Universal App

If you have an iPhone, iPad and Apple Watch, WaterMinder will synch your data across all of your devices.
